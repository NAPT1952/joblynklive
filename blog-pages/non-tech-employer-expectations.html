What Indian Employers Really Look for in Non-Tech Candidates in 2026

1. Reliability - Be on time, complete tasks

2. Communication - Speak clearly, listen carefully

3. Positive Attitude - No complaining, show energy

4. Adaptability - Say "I can learn"

5. Problem Solving - Stay calm, solve issues

6. Grooming - Clean attire, good posture

7. Stability - Show job tenure

8. Computer Skills - Email, Excel basics

9. Honesty - Never lie on resume

10. Energy - Show enthusiasm
