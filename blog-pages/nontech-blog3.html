Networking Strategies for Job Seekers in India

By JobLynk Team | April 16, 2024 | 5 min read

Why Networking Matters

Over 70% of jobs are never advertised publicly. They are filled through referrals and networking. Building connections is essential.

1. Optimize Your LinkedIn Profile

Professional photo, keyword-rich headline, detailed experience section. Turn on "Open to Work" feature.

2. Connect with Recruiters

Search for HR managers and recruiters in your industry. Send personalized connection requests.

3. Engage with Content

Like, comment, and share posts from companies you want to work for. Recruiters notice active profiles.

4. Attend Industry Events

Webinars, conferences, and meetups (virtual or in-person). Collect business cards and follow up.

5. Informational Interviews

Request 15-minute calls with professionals in your target role. Ask about their journey and advice.

Sample LinkedIn Message

"Hi [Name], I came across your profile and was impressed by your journey in [industry]. I am currently looking for opportunities in [role] and would love to learn from your experience. Would you be open to a quick 10-minute call?"
