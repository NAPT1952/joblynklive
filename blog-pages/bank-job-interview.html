Bank Job Interview Scripts for Non-Tech Roles in India

By JobLynk Team | April 16, 2024 | 7 min read

Key BFSI Interview Questions With Scripts

1. Tell me about yourself.

I am a commerce graduate with 3 years of experience in retail banking. I handled 100+ customer accounts and met branch targets.

2. Why do you want to work in banking?

Banking offers structured career growth and clear performance metrics.

3. How do you handle an upset customer?

I listen carefully, acknowledge the inconvenience, and take immediate action.

4. Are you comfortable with sales targets?

Yes. I see targets as motivation and focus on consultative selling.

Salary Benchmarks

  • Bank PO: 3.8 - 5 LPA
  • Relationship Manager: 4 - 8 LPA + incentives
  • Credit Analyst: 4.5 - 9 LPA
