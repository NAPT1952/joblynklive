Remote Work Best Practices for Indian IT Professionals

By JobLynk Team | April 16, 2024 | 7 min read

Introduction

Remote work is here to stay. Indian IT professionals need to adapt to succeed in a distributed work environment.

1. Create a Dedicated Workspace

Set up a quiet corner with good lighting, ergonomic chair, and reliable internet. Separate work from personal space.

2. Stick to a Routine

Wake up at the same time, get dressed, and follow a morning routine. Log in and log off at consistent times.

3. Over-communicate

Use Slack, Teams, or Zoom. Share daily updates. Ask questions. Don't wait for meetings to communicate.

4. Set Boundaries

Inform family of your work hours. Use headphones to signal you are working. Take breaks away from your desk.

5. Invest in Equipment

Good microphone, webcam, and noise-cancelling headphones make a huge difference in virtual meetings.

6. Stay Connected

Join virtual coffee chats. Participate in team building. Build relationships beyond work tasks.

Common Challenges and Solutions

Loneliness: Join remote work communities on LinkedIn or WhatsApp.
Distractions: Use Pomodoro technique (25 min work, 5 min break).
Career Growth: Proactively seek feedback and visibility with managers.
