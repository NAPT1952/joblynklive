Data Analyst Career in India

By JobLynk Team | April 17, 2024 | 7 min read

What Does a Data Analyst Do?

Data analysts collect, process, and perform statistical analyses on data. They help companies make better business decisions.

Skills Required

  • SQL
  • Excel Advanced
  • Python or R
  • Tableau or Power BI
  • Statistics

Salary Range

Entry Level: 4-8 LPA | Mid Level: 8-15 LPA | Senior: 15-25 LPA
