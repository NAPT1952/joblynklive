Smart Job Application Strategy for Indian Candidates: Apply Better, Not Just More

By JobLynk Team | April 17, 2024 | 5 min read

Many candidates apply to 300 jobs and hear nothing. The problem: poor strategy.

Best Practices for Applying

  • Customize resume for each role using keywords from JD
  • Apply within first 48 hours
  • Use LinkedIn + Job Portals + Direct Careers Page
  • Follow up professionally after 5-7 days
  • Track applications using an Excel sheet

Final Thought

Random effort gives random results. Structured effort gets interviews.
