Salary Negotiation Tips for Indian Job Market

By JobLynk Team | April 8, 2024 | 7 min read

Know Your Worth

Research salaries on Glassdoor, AmbitionBox, and LinkedIn.

Negotiation Script

"Thank you for the offer. Based on my research and experience, I was expecting a range of Y - Z. Is there flexibility to get closer to Z?"

Other Negotiable Items

  • Joining bonus
  • Health insurance for parents
  • Professional development budget
  • Additional vacation days
