About JobLynk.live

Bridging Executive Talent with Global Career Opportunities

🎯 Our Mission

JobLynk.live is a premier digital platform dedicated to bridging the gap between executive talent and career opportunities in the hospitality and maritime industries across India, the Middle East, and international markets.

Founded by hospitality executive Nigel Thomas, who brings over 20 years of global leadership experience across luxury resorts, cruise lines, and F&B operations, our mission is to provide Indian professionals with comprehensive career guides, industry insights, salary benchmarks, and direct connections to top employers.

We understand the challenges job seekers face – from crafting the perfect resume to finding the right recruiters. That's why we've created a one-stop solution that combines free career resources with affordable paid services like tailored resume writing, cover letter creation, and daily HR leads delivered directly to your WhatsApp.

🌟 Our Promise: Transparent pricing, no hidden fees, no subscriptions, and real results. Every service is designed to help you land your dream job faster.

👔 Meet the Founder – Nigel Thomas

Nigel Thomas is an accomplished hospitality executive with over 20 years of progressive leadership experience across luxury resorts, high-volume F&B operations, and international cruise lines spanning India, the Middle East, and the USA.

His expertise includes P&L management, cost optimisation, team leadership, and guest experience enhancement. Having helped hundreds of professionals navigate their career paths, Nigel created JobLynk.live to share his insights and provide practical tools for job seekers in the hospitality and maritime industries.
