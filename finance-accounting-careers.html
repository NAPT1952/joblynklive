💰 Finance & Accounting Careers in India: From Entry-Level to CFO – No Coding Required

📅 May 3, 2026 ⏱ 11 min read 🏷️ #FinanceJobs #NonTechJobs

Finance and accounting remain evergreen career fields in India. Every company from a local startup to a multinational bank needs professionals to manage books, file taxes, raise capital, and ensure compliance. With the rise of fintech, GST, and IPO mania, demand for skilled finance talent is surging. This guide covers job roles, salary ranges, certifications like CA, CPA, CMA, CFA, and how to build a successful career without writing a single line of code.

Top finance & accounting roles in India

1. Accountant / Junior Accountant

What you do: Handle ledgers, invoices, TDS, GST. Skills: Tally, Excel, GST filing. Salary: ₹2.5–5 LPA.

2. Financial Analyst - FP&A

What you do: Budgeting, forecasting, variance analysis. Skills: Excel, Financial Modeling, SQL, Power BI. Salary: ₹5–12 LPA.

3. Tax Consultant - Direct/Indirect Tax

What you do: Advise on income tax, GST compliance. Skills: Income Tax Act, GST laws, Excel. Salary: ₹6–15 LPA experienced.

4. Auditor - Internal/Statutory

What you do: Work with Big4 or mid-tier firms. Skills: Accounting Standards, Audit tools. Salary: ₹6–18 LPA depending on qualification.

5. Investment Banking Analyst

What you do: M&A, fundraising. Skills: Valuation, Financial Modeling, PowerPoint. Salary: ₹15–30 LPA top tier.

Key certifications and their ROI

Chartered Accountant - CA: Gold standard in India. 4-5 years of study, but freshers earn ₹9–25 LPA. Mandatory for signing audit reports.
CPA - Certified Public Accountant: US-focused, great for MNCs. 12-18 months. Avg salary: ₹10-25 LPA in India, $70k+ abroad.
CMA - Cost & Management Accountant: Valued in manufacturing. 3 years. Avg salary: ₹6-18 LPA.
CFA - Chartered Financial Analyst: For investment roles. 3 levels, 2-4 years. Avg salary: ₹12-30 LPA.
No certification path: Financial analyst, FP&A, accounts executive roles. Start with B.Com + Excel + SQL. Salary: ₹4-12 LPA. Many reach ₹20 LPA+ without CA via experience.
💡 No CA? No problem: Many finance professionals start with B.Com + internships, then pursue part-time certifications like US CMA or MBA Finance. Experience often outweighs degrees in mid-level roles.
